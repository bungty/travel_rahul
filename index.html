Yamunotri Temple Pilgrimage

Yamunotri is the sacred source of the Yamuna River and one of the four holy Char Dham sites in Uttarakhand. Surrounded by snow-covered peaks, hot springs, and divine serenity, it is a place of deep spiritual energy.

  • Scenic Himalayan mountain views
  • Hot springs: Surya Kund & Gauri Kund
  • Spiritual Char Dham experience
  • Easy access via Max Van & road travel

Gangotri Temple Pilgrimage

Gangotri is the sacred origin of the River Ganga and one of the four holy Char Dham pilgrimage destinations. Surrounded by majestic Himalayan peaks, it is revered for its divine aura, spiritual depth, and breathtaking natural scenery.

  • Majestic Himalayan mountain scenery
  • Sacred origin of River Ganga
  • Trekking trails & spiritual paths
  • Easy travel via Max Van & road

Kedarnath Temple Pilgrimage

Kedarnath is one of the holiest shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva and a sacred Char Dham destination. Nestled in the high Himalayas, it offers a divine spiritual journey amidst breathtaking mountain landscapes and deep religious significance.

  • Sacred Lord Shiva Temple
  • Stunning Himalayan mountain views
  • Trekking route from Gaurikund
  • Max Van access till Gaurikund

Badrinath Temple Pilgrimage

Badrinath is one of the most sacred Vishnu temples and a revered Char Dham pilgrimage destination. Located between the Nar and Narayan peaks, it offers divine blessings, spiritual peace, and breathtaking Himalayan scenery.

  • Sacred Vishnu Temple
  • Located between Nar & Narayan peaks
  • Easy access via road & short treks
  • Max Van travel supported till Badrinath
